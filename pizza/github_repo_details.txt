# 🍕 Pizza Sales Insights Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project demonstrates how raw business data can be transformed into actionable insights using **Power BI**.  
It is based on a case study of a mid-sized pizza chain and highlights how data-driven decision-making improves revenue, efficiency, and customer targeting.

## 🚀 Key Features
- **Total Revenue, Orders & Pizzas Sold Tracking**
- **Monthly Sales & Order Trends**
- **Top & Bottom Selling Pizzas (by quantity & revenue)**
- **Category-wise Revenue Breakdown (Classic, Supreme, Chicken, Veggie)**
- **Customer Behavior Analysis (time of day & day of week)**
- **Predictive Analytics Layer for Sales Growth (+7.51%)**

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI Desktop**
- Data Visualization (Charts, Graphs, Dashboards)
- Predictive Analytics (Basic Forecasting)

## 💡 Business Impact
- Identified top-performing products (e.g., Thai Chicken, Pepperoni, Hawaiian)
- Optimized marketing campaigns and reduced waste from low performers
- Improved staff scheduling by aligning with peak times (7–9 PM, weekends)
- Enabled **data-backed seasonal and growth strategies**
